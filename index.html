What We Do

Content is a social news application that brings your favorite content to you. Your network of friends and influencers curates your feed, so you can have the most relevant content delivered to your Paper. Explore new content by subscribing to RSS feeds of news channels and content creators, and share your favorites with your network.

Our Mission

We are providing a platform for you and your network to organize the internet’s content through increasing the accessibility of quality online information. We recognize the harmful effects of spending excessive amounts of time on traditional social media sites, and we hope our app will be able to create better, more informative online experiences. We aim to build a more informed society by making the consumption of relevant content easy and enjoyable.

Who It's For

Our target audience is any adult who needs a better way to keep up with current events and consume content in today’s growing amount of online information.
